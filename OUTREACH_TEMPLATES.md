## Outreach Strategy

**Phase 1: Warm Outreach (Weeks 1-2)**
- 50 personalized emails to QA leads at target companies
- Response time: 3-5 days
- Target: 5-10 replies
- Goal: 2-3 demos scheduled

**Phase 2: Demo & Qualification (Weeks 3-4)**
- Deliver demos to interested prospects
- Identify "hot" leads (ready to pilot)
- Goal: 1-2 pilot engagements

**Phase 3: Pilot Execution (Weeks 5-8)**
- Run 2-week pilot with 1-2 early customers
- Document results (bugs found, time saved)
- Turn into case study

**Phase 4: Scale (Weeks 9-12)**
- Use case study in outreach
- Launch Product Hunt
- Post on Hacker News
- Goal: 50+ inbound inquiries

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO:
- Personalize every email (mention their company/product)
- Lead with value (what problem do you solve?)
- Quantify results (numbers beat adjectives)
- Make it easy to say yes (demo link, calendar link)
- Follow up 1-2 times (persistence pays)
- Share case studies (social proof matters)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Send generic emails ("Hi [Company]")
- Lead with "we're hiring" or "check out our blog"
- Make big claims without evidence
- Send attachments (use links instead)
- Follow up more than 3 times (be respectful)
- Sell the demo (qualify first, pitch second)
