# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 5, 2024
**Effective Date:** July 5, 2024

Zero to Test AI ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, company name, phone number
- **Profile Information:** GitHub username, deployment preferences
- **Usage Data:** Test scripts, test results, code snippets you upload
- **Communication:** Emails, support tickets, feedback forms

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
- **Usage Analytics:** Features used, test runs executed, time spent
- **Device Information:** OS, browser version, device type
- **Cookies:** Session cookies, preferences, analytics

### 1.3 Third-Party Information
- **GitHub Integration:** Repository data, issue titles, commits (only what you authorize)
- **Cloud Providers:** Deployment logs if you use AWS/GCP/Azure integration

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
- Process your test runs and generate reports
- Send service updates and security alerts
- Respond to support requests
- Analyze usage patterns and improve performance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse

**We do NOT:**
- Sell personal data to third parties
- Use your test data for AI model training (without explicit consent)
- Share test results with competitors or unauthorized parties

## 3. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while account is active + 30 days after deletion
- **Test results:** Retained per your configuration (default: 90 days)
- **Log data:** Retained for 30 days
- **Backups:** May be retained longer for disaster recovery

You can request deletion of your data at any time via support@zerototestai.com

## 4. Data Security

We implement:
- TLS 1.2+ encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Incident response procedures

**However:** No security measure is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Correction:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Deletion:** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Portability:** Receive data in portable format
- **Right to Object:** Opt-out of certain processing

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@zerototestai.com

## 6. GDPR Compliance (EU Residents)

If you're in the EU:
- We process data on basis of contractual necessity and legitimate interests
- We have Data Processing Agreements with subprocessors
- We respect all GDPR rights listed above
- We conduct Data Impact Assessments
- For international transfers, we use Standard Contractual Clauses

## 7. CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

If you're in California:
- You have the right to know what personal information is collected
- You have the right to delete personal information
- You have the right to opt-out of "sales" (we don't sell data)
- You have the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware we've collected such information, we'll delete it immediately.

## 9. Third-Party Services

Our Services may integrate with:
- **GitHub:** For repository access (governed by GitHub's privacy policy)
- **AWS/GCP/Azure:** For cloud deployment (governed by their policies)
- **Stripe:** For payment processing (governed by Stripe's privacy policy)

We're not responsible for their privacy practices.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll notify you of material changes via:
- Email to your account email
- Prominent notice on our website
- In-app notification

Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.
